# Aino — Finnish language tutor

Aino is a conversational Finnish language tutor that teaches through natural dialogue and immersive storytelling rather than grammar drills. Built on Claude (Anthropic) with voice input and output.

## What it does

**Conversation mode** — chat with Aino in scenarios like ordering at a café, navigating transport, or arriving at a gig venue. She introduces Finnish naturally, corrects gently, and builds your vocabulary through context.

**Immersive stories** — based on the Comprehensible Input method, Aino tells short, vivid stories in Finnish with emoji scaffolding so you can understand meaning without translation. A bear addicted to coffee. A cat who sleeps in a hat. Choose from five difficulty levels.

**Voice** — click any Finnish word to hear its pronunciation. Hover to see the English meaning. Use your microphone to speak Finnish back.

## Features

- Conversational learning through natural dialogue
- Immersive CI stories with emoji-based comprehension at 5 levels
- Click-to-hear pronunciation on any Finnish word
- Hover-to-translate on vocabulary
- Voice input via microphone (Chrome/Edge)
- Comprehension check-ins after stories
- Session vocabulary tracking
- Dark and light themes

## Tech

Next.js 14, Anthropic Claude API (Sonnet), Web Speech API, deployed on Vercel.
